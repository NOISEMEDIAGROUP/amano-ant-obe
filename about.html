Our Story

AMANO started in Berlin in 2009 with a simple idea.

A hotel should feel like the city it lives in.

We did not start with rooms. We started with a bar. Then a restaurant. Then the kind of nights that made people want to come back -- and eventually, stay.

Today there are twelve AMANO hotels across Berlin and London. Each one is shaped by the neighbourhood, the people, and the energy around it. Not a formula. A response.

The Local Test

"Great hotels are not made credible by tourists. They are made credible by the locals."

Before we open a bar, host an event, or launch a partnership, we ask three questions: Which pillar does this serve? Does it make locals want to come? Is it a city story, not a hotel ad?

If the answer is no, we rethink.

Sustainability

Honest steps,
not headlines.

AMANO holds Green Key certification across its properties. That means verified standards for energy, water, waste, and responsible sourcing -- audited annually, not self-declared.

We are not going to claim we have solved everything. But we are committed to measurable progress: reducing single-use plastics, sourcing locally where we can, and being transparent about where we fall short.

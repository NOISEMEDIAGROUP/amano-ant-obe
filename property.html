The London Chapter

Berlin spirit.
London address.

AMANO Covent Garden is what happens when Berlin's instinct for atmosphere, design and late-night energy meets London's cultural density. The theatre district is at the door. Soho is a short walk. The Thames is ten minutes south.

The hotel does not compete with any of that. It plugs into it.
